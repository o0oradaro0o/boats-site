Battleships is a game heavily based on positioning, team coordination, and map awareness. Dive headfirst into enemy lines with high DPS, short range weapons or pick off enemies from afar with low DPS, high range weapons.

Navigate the perilous seas as a Trader and use the economy to your advantage.

Use stealth and trickery to slip through the enemy lines and secure lucrative trading contracts. Tilt the odds in your favor by funding your team's war efforts.
